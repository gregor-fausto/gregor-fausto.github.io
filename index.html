I am a PhD student in the Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at Cornell University. I study plant populations and try to understand the causes and consequences of variation in life histories.

I am working on a project that incorporates environmental variables and density-dependence into demographic models to predict abundance. I am working with my advisor, Monica Geber, and collaborator Bill Morris.

I also study how plant development interacts with variation in resource availability and season length to shape life history trade-offs.

Keywords: population ecology, community ecology, demography, evolutionary ecology, coexistence theory
