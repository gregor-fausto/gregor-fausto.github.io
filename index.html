I am a PhD student in the Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at Cornell University. I study plant populations and communities to understand the causes and consequences of genetic and species diversity. I am also working with my advisor, Monica Geber, and collaborator Bill Morris on a project that incorporates environmental variables and density-dependence into demographic models to predict abundance.

Keywords population and community ecology, demography, coexistence theory
